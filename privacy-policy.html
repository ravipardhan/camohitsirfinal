Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which M S Sharma & Co. (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users (“User”) of the website https://www.mssharmaco.in (“Site”).

This Privacy Policy applies to the Site and all products and services offered by M S Sharma & Co.

1. Personal Identification Information

We may collect personal identification information from Users in a variety of ways, including, but not limited to: when Users visit our Site, register on the Site, place an order or make a payment, subscribe to newsletters, or use any other features, services, or resources made available.

Users may be asked for details such as name, email address, mailing address, phone number, PAN, Aadhaar, or incorporation details.

Users may visit our Site anonymously. We will collect personal identification information only if voluntarily submitted. However, refusal to provide certain information may prevent Users from availing specific Site-related services.

2. Non-Personal Identification Information

We may collect non-personal identification information about Users whenever they interact with our Site. This may include browser type, device and operating system, internet service provider, IP address and other technical information.

3. Web Browser Cookies

Our Site may use “cookies” to enhance User experience. Cookies are placed on a User’s device for record-keeping and tracking purposes. Users can choose to set their browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of the Site may not function properly in such cases.

4. How We Use Collected Information

M S Sharma & Co. may use collected information for purposes including:

  • Improving customer service – to respond to requests more efficiently.
  • Personalizing User experience – analyzing aggregate data to understand usage patterns.
  • Improving our Site – using feedback to enhance services.
  • Processing payments – using information solely for the order placed, not sharing beyond what is necessary.
  • Marketing & updates – sending newsletters, updates, or service-related communication (if User opts-in).
  • Legal & compliance – fulfilling obligations under Indian tax, company, and compliance laws.

5. How We Protect Your Information

We adopt appropriate data collection, storage, and security practices to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of personal information, username, password, transaction information, and data stored on our Site.

All sensitive and private data exchange happens over SSL secured channels. The Site complies with industry security standards for data protection.

6. Sharing Your Personal Information

We may share limited information with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our business, conducting surveys, sending newsletters, or processing payments—only to the extent necessary.

Users acknowledge and authorize that their information may be shared with:

  • Government authorities (GST, MCA, Income Tax, Trademark Registry, etc.) as part of service delivery.
  • Our sister/affiliate concerns for providing integrated professional services.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or restructuring, User information may be transferred, with confidentiality maintained.

7. Links to Other Sites

Our Site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of such third-party sites. Users are encouraged to review their privacy policies before sharing information.

8. Google AdSense & Analytics

Ads served on our Site may be delivered by Google or other partners. Google’s DART cookie enables ads based on User visits.

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are designed for adult individuals and businesses who require tax, compliance, and professional advisory services. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that such information has been inadvertently collected, we will delete it from our records immediately.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in law, technology, or our business practices. Updated versions will always be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.

For material changes, we may notify you through email or a notice on our Site. By continuing to use our services after an update, you confirm your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
