📘 Private Limited Company Registration in India – Made Simple for You

When you think of starting or growing your business, the first step is choosing the right structure. A Private Limited Company (Pvt. Ltd.), governed by the Companies Act, 2013, is one of the most trusted and widely chosen business setups in India.

It gives your venture a separate legal identity, protects your personal assets from risks, and enhances trust with clients, suppliers, and investors. In short, it builds a strong foundation for your business, your growth, your ease.

  • Perfect for startups, growing enterprises, and businesses planning to raise funds.
  • Requires a minimum of 2 Directors and 2 Shareholders (same individuals can be both).

✅ Why Choose Private Limited Company Registration for Your Business?

  1. Legal Recognition – Your company becomes a separate legal entity, able to own property, enter into contracts, and safeguard your interests.
  2. Limited Liability – Your personal assets are safe; your liability is limited to the shares you hold.
  3. Funding Opportunities – Attract investors, venture capitalists, and business partners with credibility.
  4. Business Continuity – Your company runs seamlessly despite changes in directors/shareholders.
  5. Government Benefits – Access to tax benefits, startup schemes, and easier compliance advantages.

📜 Governing Laws You Don’t Need to Worry About (We Handle Them for You)

  • Companies Act, 2013 – For incorporation, governance, and closure.
  • Income Tax Act, 1961 – For taxation and compliance.
  • GST Laws – If your business crosses the turnover threshold.
  • FEMA & RBI Guidelines – For businesses with foreign investment.

👉 We simplify compliance so you can focus on what matters — growing your business.

🏢 Types of Private Limited Companies

  1. Company Limited by Shares – Liability limited to unpaid shares.
  2. Company Limited by Guarantee – Members commit to a fixed sum if the company winds up.
  3. Unlimited Company – No limit on liability but still a separate legal entity.

🎯 Who Can Register a Pvt. Ltd. Company?

  • ✔ Minimum 2 Directors (1 must be Indian resident).
  • ✔ Minimum 2 Shareholders.
  • ✔ Registered Office in India (owned/rented with NOC).
  • ✔ No minimum capital required.

📑 Documents Required – Simple Checklist for You

For Directors & Shareholders

  • PAN & Aadhaar (for Indian nationals)
  • Address proof (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Linked Mobile & Email ID

For Registered Office

  • Proof of address (electricity/tax bill)
  • Rent/lease agreement
  • NOC from property owner

🏆 Our Process – Quick & Hassle-Free

  1. Collect your documents.
  2. Apply for DSC & DIN.
  3. Reserve your company name.
  4. Draft MOA & AOA.
  5. File incorporation forms with ROC.
  6. Receive your Certificate of Incorporation (COI).

📅 Timeframe: 7–10 working days.
