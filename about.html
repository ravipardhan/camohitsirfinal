MEET OUR FOUNDER

Driven by Passion for Reducing Compliance Stress

CS Mohit Shyam Sharma brings his 14+ years of experience in dealing with startups, corporates and professionals for tax, corporate law, and accounting to lead our mission forward. His vision: make professional compliance as easy and empowering as managing your business.

CS Mohit Shyam Sharma is a qualified Company Secretary and a registered Trademark Agent, bringing comprehensive expertise in corporate laws, regulatory compliance, and intellectual property matters.

With a strong foundation in secretarial practice, he specializes in handling end-to-end compliance for companies, LLPs, and startups—ensuring that businesses remain legally sound and operationally efficient. His areas of practice include ROC compliances, company incorporation, corporate restructuring, trademark registration, and ongoing regulatory advisory.

Known for his practical approach and attention to detail, CS Mohit Shyam Sharma focuses on simplifying complex legal procedures and providing clear, actionable guidance to clients. He is also a strong advocate of technology-driven compliance, focusing on minimizing manual intervention through smart tools, automation, and streamlined digital processes.
