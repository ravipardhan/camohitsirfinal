Refund Policy

At M S Sharma & Co. we take immense pride in delivering accurate, reliable, and professional GST, taxation, and compliance services. Your satisfaction is our top priority, and we are committed to resolving any concerns you may have with our services.

If at any stage you are not satisfied with our work, please reach out to us immediately. Our team will make every effort to resolve the issue, provide a suitable credit adjustment for future services, or process a refund (as applicable under this policy).

Not Satisfied? Let Us Know!

If you feel that the service delivered does not meet your expectations, please contact us using the same email ID through which the payment was made. Our support team will escalate your concern to the respective professional handling your case, ensuring prompt resolution.

  • Delays or rejections from Government departments (GST, MCA, Income Tax, Trademark Registry, etc.)

We request your patience as our team works to complete your engagement with the best possible outcome.

If you wish to switch the service you purchased for another service offered by M S Sharma & Co., you may request this change within 7 days of your payment.
